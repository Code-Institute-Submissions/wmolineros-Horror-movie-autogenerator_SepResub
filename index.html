Horror Movie Assessor

Want to find the best horro movie to watch for your time of year? From low to high scare level, this game has got you covered.


Rules of the game

  • Select the "Start Game" button to begin the game
  • You will need to make a selection from 10 questions that will lead you to a couple of suggestions.
  • You can make your selection simply by clicking from the options available.
  • There are no right or wrong answers, this game is totally curated for your needs.
  • At the end, you will have a couple of suggestions that fit your criteria the best.
  • Now to close the rules and continue with the assessment, select "x" or go to "Home Sweet Home".
